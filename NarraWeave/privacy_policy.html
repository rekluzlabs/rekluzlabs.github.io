NarraWeave Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026  ·  Version 1.3

At Rekluz Labs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how NarraWeave collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

NarraWeave personalizes your experience using data available on your device. This information is stored locally on your device only — Rekluz Labs does not collect, receive, or store this data on any server:

  • Personal Profile: Information you provide such as your name, age, and occupation.
  • Contextual Data: Your device's approximate location (GPS), current weather conditions, and local time of day.
  • Story Progress: Your generated chapters and story preferences.

2. How This Data Is Used

This data is used exclusively to generate personalized story content. When you request a new chapter, the App sends your profile and contextual data directly from your device to the AI provider you have configured (see Section 3) to generate that content. Rekluz Labs does not process, view, or store this data — the App facilitates the connection, and the AI provider performs the generation.

3. Data Transmission to AI Providers

Story generation features transmit user inputs and contextual data (location, weather, time) to third-party AI providers. Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for how they handle this data. These providers include:

  • Google (Gemini API)
  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • OpenAI (GPT models)
  • Cohere

We do not store your personally identifiable contextual data on our own servers. The data is sent directly from your device to the AI provider to generate the chapter.

4. Data Storage

Most of your data, including your profile and story history, is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app or clear its data, this information is permanently removed unless you have exported it.

5. Your Choices

You can control the collection of contextual data at any time through the App's settings. You may choose to disable GPS, weather, or time-of-day integration. You can also modify or delete your player profile whenever you wish.

6. Age Requirements and Children's Privacy

NarraWeave is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 16. NarraWeave stores all data locally on the user's device and does not transmit personal data to our own servers, so Rekluz Labs has no ability to remotely access, retrieve, or delete data from any device. If a child under 16 has used NarraWeave, a parent or guardian can remove all associated data at any time by deleting the App or clearing its data through device settings.

During setup, users confirm their age. Users who confirm they are 18 years of age or older will have access to mature content, which may include more intense themes. This age confirmation is self-reported and stored locally on the device; we do not independently verify age.
