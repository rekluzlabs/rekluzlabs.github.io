Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026 | Version 1.3

At Rekluz Labs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how NarraWeave collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

NarraWeave is designed to personalize your experience using data available on your device:

  • Personal Profile: Information you provide such as your name, age, and occupation.
  • Contextual Data: Your device's approximate location (GPS), current weather conditions, and local time of day.
  • Story Progress: Your generated chapters and story preferences.
  • User Input (Topics): Any text you enter into optional topic boxes to guide story generation.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to generate personalized story content. We use contextual data to shape the narrative environment and player profile data to make the protagonist feel uniquely like you.

3. Data Transmission to AI Providers

Story generation features transmit user-provided inputs to third-party AI providers to generate narratives. This data includes:

  • Story Topics: Your thoughts and ideas shared via the Topic box.
  • Personal Profile: Details from your protagonist profile.
  • Contextual Data: Real-time location, weather, and time (if enabled).

Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for how they handle this data. NarraWeave supports several providers, including:

  • Google (Gemini)
  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • OpenAI (GPT models)
  • Mistral AI
  • DeepSeek
  • xAI (Grok)
  • Cohere
  • OpenRouter (Meta Llama, etc.)

We do not store your personally identifiable contextual data on our own servers. The data is sent directly from your device to the selected AI provider.

4. Data Storage

Most of your data, including your profile and story history, is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app or clear its data, this information is permanently removed unless you have exported it.

5. Your Choices

You can control the collection of contextual data at any time through the App's settings. You may choose to disable GPS, weather, or time-of-day integration. You can also modify or delete your player profile whenever you wish.

6. Children's Privacy

NarraWeave is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 16. If we become aware that a user under 16 is using NarraWeave, we will take steps to delete their data and restrict access to the App.
