NarraWeave Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026 · Version 1.4

At Rekluz Labs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how NarraWeave collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our application.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

NarraWeave personalizes your experience using data available on your device. This information is stored locally on your device only — Rekluz Labs does not collect, receive, or store this data on any server. The app stores your personal profile (name, age, occupation), contextual data (GPS location, weather, time of day), story progress and preferences, and any topic inputs you provide to guide generation. All of this remains on your device under your control.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to generate personalized story content within the app. When you request a new chapter, NarraWeave uses your profile and contextual data to craft a narrative environment tailored to you, making the protagonist feel uniquely like you.

Important: Rekluz Labs does not process, view, access, or store any of this data. Your device manages all data locally.

3. Data Transmission to Your Selected AI Provider

To generate stories, NarraWeave sends data directly from your device to the AI provider you have configured. You control which provider you use at any time.

Critical Point: Although Rekluz Labs does not collect your data, your personal profile information (name, age, occupation), contextual data (location, weather, time), and story topic inputs are transmitted to your chosen AI provider to generate your story. Each provider handles this data according to their own privacy policies. You are responsible for reviewing those policies before selecting a provider.

Supported AI Providers: NarraWeave supports a range of AI providers including Google (Gemini), Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT models), Mistral AI, DeepSeek, xAI (Grok), Cohere, and OpenRouter (Meta Llama and others). You select which provider to use when configuring the app, and you can change this selection at any time.

Each provider handles your transmitted data according to their own privacy policies. Please review the privacy documentation for any provider before selecting them. Major providers include Google (https://policies.google.com/privacy), Anthropic (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy), and OpenAI (https://openai.com/privacy). For other providers, check their websites for privacy information.

4. Story Sharing Feature

NarraWeave includes a share feature that allows you to share generated stories through standard online platforms like email, messaging apps, and social media. When you use the share feature, you have complete control over what content is shared and to whom. Rekluz Labs does not facilitate or manage this sharing—the app uses your device's native share sheet to transmit content to your chosen platform or contact.

Once content is shared to a third-party platform, that platform's terms and privacy policies apply. Rekluz Labs is not responsible for how social media services, email providers, messaging apps, or other platforms handle, store, or use the content you share with them. Review those services' privacy policies if you have concerns about shared data.

5. Data Storage and Retention

All your data, including your profile and story history, is stored locally on your device. Rekluz Labs has no servers, databases, or backups of your information. If you delete the app or clear its data through your device settings, this information is permanently removed from your device. The only exception is if you have explicitly exported your stories to an external location.

6. Your Choices and Control

You have complete control over your data at all times. You can disable contextual data collection—GPS, weather, or time-of-day integration—at any time through the app's settings. You can modify or delete your player profile whenever you wish, and you can delete individual stories or clear all story progress from the settings menu. If you want to switch to a different AI provider or change your API keys, you can do so at any point without restriction.

7. Age Requirements and Children's Privacy

NarraWeave is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly allow use by users under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 16.

Age Confirmation: During setup, users confirm their age. Users who confirm they are 18 years of age or older will have access to mature content, which may include more intense themes. This age confirmation is self-reported and stored locally on the device; we do not independently verify age.

Data Deletion for Minors: Since all data is stored locally on your device, Rekluz Labs has no ability to remotely access, retrieve, or delete data from any device. If a child under 16 has used NarraWeave, a parent or guardian can remove all associated data at any time by deleting the app or clearing its data through device settings.

8. Security

NarraWeave is designed with privacy and security as core principles. By storing all data locally on your device rather than on remote servers, we minimize exposure to network-based attacks. Your API keys for AI providers are stored securely using your device's built-in encryption through Android Keystore. NarraWeave does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries—there are no third-party services monitoring your activity or profile.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of NarraWeave after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
