Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Akachan's Adventures by Rekluz Labs

1. Introduction

Rekluz Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the mobile game Akachan's Adventures. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application distributed through the Google Play Store. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Akachan's Adventures is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

2. Information We Collect

We collect little to no personal information.

2.1 Information You Provide

Support & Feedback: If you contact us by email, we may receive your message and any information you choose to include (such as your email address).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Akachan's Adventures does not actively collect or transmit personal data. However, limited technical information may be generated locally on your device for app functionality, such as:

  • Basic device and system information (used only for compatibility during runtime)
  • Temporary error logs stored on your device (not transmitted to us)

This information is not sent to our servers.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number) unless you contact us directly
  • Location data
  • Contacts, messages, or files
  • Gameplay analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Crash reports sent to external services
  • Payment or financial information
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data

3. How We Use Information

Any information you voluntarily provide (such as via email) is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or support requests
  • Improve the app based on your feedback

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Akachan's Adventures does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • The app does not transmit personal data to external servers
  • Any information you send via email is handled securely and only used for support purposes
  • We take reasonable measures to protect any information we receive

6. Data Retention

Support Emails: Retained only as long as necessary to respond to your request, then deleted upon request or no longer needed. No other user data is stored by us.

7. Children’s Privacy

Akachan's Adventures is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is provided, it will be deleted upon request.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of any information you have provided
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Since we do not maintain user accounts or stored personal data, access and portability requests are limited to information you have directly provided.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
