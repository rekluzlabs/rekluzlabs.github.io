Mission Statement

Independent Android software, built without compromise

Rekluz Labs is an independent Android software studio committed to building applications that are private by architecture, not by policy. Privacy is treated as a design constraint, embedded into how our software is built rather than added later or monetized.

```

Across our applications, this principle is reflected in concrete ways: user data is processed locally where possible, or routed directly through user-controlled services rather than our own infrastructure. We avoid advertising networks, third-party trackers, and behavioral analytics. Any information voluntarily provided by users is used solely for direct communication and retained only as long as necessary for that purpose.

All applications are distributed free of charge, with no advertisements, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. This places us at odds with prevailing industry models that prioritize engagement and monetization over user benefit. We deliberately reject those incentives in favor of building software that serves the user without manipulation or compromise.

We approach development as a discipline of deliberate, well-considered decisions. Each product is built with consistent attention to security, performance, and usability, and tested against real-world conditions rather than abstract benchmarks. Technical decisions and their tradeoffs are documented transparently.

As a solo-operated studio, Rekluz Labs maintains direct ownership over every layer of development, from system architecture to user experience. This structure ensures that technical and ethical decisions remain aligned. Thoughtful design and sound engineering are treated as the same principle, expressed at different layers of the product.

Our objective is simple: to demonstrate that mobile software can be free of cost, free of advertising, and uncompromising in its respect for user privacy — while still meeting a high standard of technical quality and design.
```
